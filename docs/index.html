Applyly™

The intelligent, privacy-first companion that streamlines your job application journey.

Experience Seamless Precision

Intelligent Parsing

Harness advanced extraction logic to transform your PDF and DOCX resumes into a structured, searchable data engine. Every detail—from GPA to complex project bullet points—is mapped with surgical precision.

Smart Autofill

Bypass common form-filling roadblocks. Our engine intelligently identifies fields even within complex iframes and Shadow DOM environments, ensuring a smooth one-click experience on LinkedIn, Greenhouse, and Lever.

Manual Entry Helper

For forms requiring a manual touch, our systematic sidekick organizes your data into clean, copyable categories. Access granular date components and specific field values instantly without switching tabs.

Privacy First

Your career data is sensitive and stays that way. Applyly operates with a local-only architecture—your profiles and personal information never leave your device, ensuring total control and security.

Profile Management

Tailor your presence for every opportunity. Create and manage multiple application profiles to switch between different resume versions seamlessly, matching the right skills to the right role.

MIT Open Source

Transparent, community-driven, and permanently free. Built by engineers for engineers, Applyly is a public project dedicated to removing the friction from professional growth.

Getting Started

  1. Clone the repository from GitHub.
  2. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/ in your browser.
  3. Enable **Developer mode** using the toggle in the top right corner.
  4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the dist directory within your project folder.
